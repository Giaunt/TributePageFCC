Robert Karrfalt

Making Tree Gentics Cool Since 1972

Here's a time line of Robert Karrfalt's Career:

  • 1972 - Graduated with Masters Degree in Genetics from University of Wisconsin
  • 1975 - Hired to work for the National Tree Seed Labrotory in Dry Branch, GA
  • 1986 - Becomes director of National Tree Seed Labrotory
  • 1998 - Co-founder of the RNGR Group.
  • 2000 - Moves to Lafayette, IN for a joint program between Purdue University and the USDA Forest Service called Martel Forest Hardwood Center.
  • 2000 - Recognized as one of the most renowed plant gentists in the world of Foresty.
  • 2008 - Co-Authors Woody Tree Seed Manual
  • 2018 - Retires at the age of 70 and moves to New York State onto 40 acre wooded lot.
